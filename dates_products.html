Dates Paste

It is the best known among date derivatives. This is the pulp of crushed dates to obtain a smooth paste that can be used to prepare pastries, and as an alternative to other artificial sugary products.

Dates Jam

The date jam is a puree made of fresh date slices, flavored with cinnamon, with its natural healthy sugary taste, it can be used as a good alternative for jams that contain added artificial sugar.

Rich in trace elements, vitamins, and fiber, the date jam is ideally consumed at breakfast for a boost of energy in the morning. It is perfect for children as well as active people and athletes, with the important energy intake it provides throughout the day.

Dates Syrup

Called dates honey as well, the date syrup is based on a very concentrated date puree. Its consistency resembles honey. It is very energetic, rich in trace elements, vitamins, and natural sugars. A perfect natural sweetener that can be added to drinks, pastries, and other recipes.

Dates Sugar

Made from dried dates and very fine grinding, dates sugar brings flavor and aroma to your creams and pastries. Dates sugar is recommended by health professionals as the perfect alternative to white sugar, with its natural composition, it is considered the best addition to your daily food.

Coffee Dates Kernels

Very unique alternative for coffee, highly rich in minerals and rich soluble and insoluble fibers. The high worldwide demand for coffee requires finding alternatives, that are available, healthy, and affordable.

Dates Vinegar

With a traditional production by double fermentation, Dates vinegar is a natural product full of benefits for the treatment of many health problems for its medicinal properties, the product is very rich in nutrients including vitamins and minerals such as magnesium, iron, silicon, calcium, phosphorus, sulfur in addition to enzymes, essential acids and pectin.

Perfect addition to foods and salads, its special sweet taste of the date, will give you a unique, healthy and delicious, experience.
