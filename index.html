ABOUT US


  • We offer you premium quality of dates and dates derived products "100% natural" from the heart of the Algeria Sahara.
  • Our palm grove is located in the region of Tolga, Biskra in Algeria, among the best soils for dates in the region of North Africa.
  • We are keen to introduce the best existing quality of this prestigious fruit, "Deglet Nour" or "fingers of light", characterized by its transparent shape, revealing its nucleus. in addition to a range of organic products.
  • We give you the chance to experience the original "queen of dates", directly from the oasis to you.

What We Do

To preserve the unique and optimal taste of the queen of dates, the fruit is collected between the period of October and November. After, our dates are stored manually with the necessary delicacy. In a 100% organic process, a range of natural products are derived from the dates as Low-Sugar Alternatives to Your Favorite Foods. We are committed to providing you with the highest quality for the best competitive price in the market, our services are offered to more than 68 countries in the world. Flexible, available and responsive, are our ethics to give the highest priority for the satisfaction of our costumers.
